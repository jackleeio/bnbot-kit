## Detailed Description

BNBot is the #1 AI-powered personal branding agent for X.

🔥 BNBot (Brand & Bot) – Discover trends. Create content. Grow your brand.

❤️ What is BNBot?

BNBot is an AI-powered Chrome extension that turns X into your personal branding command center. It injects a smart sidebar into X that helps you discover trending content from 30+ platforms, generate tweets and threads with AI, automate engagement, and publish — all without leaving your browser.

Think of it as your AI social media editor — it discovers what's trending across platforms like TikTok, YouTube, Reddit and 30+ more, then helps you turn it into high-quality X content. You decide, it publishes.

⸻

🌐 BNBot Chrome Extension – What Can It Do?

• 🔍 Discover – Trending Content from 30+ Platforms
Go beyond X. BNBot fetches trending topics, viral videos, and hot content from major social networks, video platforms, tech communities, news outlets, and finance sites. All data is fetched using your browser's login session — no API keys, no setup.

• ✍️ Create – AI-Powered Content Generation
Turn trending data into tweets, threads, and long-form articles. AI generates drafts based on what's hot right now. Auto-edit videos with bnbot-cli. Supports images, videos, and media attachments.

• 📤 Publish – Auto-Post to X with 70+ Actions
Auto-post tweets, threads with media, and long articles. Schedule posts for optimal timing. Like, retweet, quote, bookmark, follow/unfollow, and more — 70+ X actions at your fingertips.

• 🤖 Automate – Smart Reply, Auto-Like, Notification Processing
Smart reply with AI that reads context and generates natural responses. Auto-like, auto-retweet, and auto-process notifications. Human behavior simulation with randomized delays. Daily limits and smart filters to stay safe.

• 📊 Analyze – Track Your Growth
Engagement metrics, follower trends, content performance, and exposure prediction. Interactive charts to visualize your growth over time.

• 🎬 Video – Auto-Edit and Publish
Pair with bnbot-cli to auto-clip trending videos, add subtitles, and publish directly to X. From discovery to publication in one workflow.

⸻

📡 30+ Platform Trend Discovery

Supports major social networks, video platforms, tech forums, developer communities, news outlets, finance sites, and podcast platforms. Browser-based scraping uses your existing login session. Additional platforms available via bnbot-cli (command-line tool).

All scraping runs locally in your browser. Your data stays on your device.

⸻

🔒 About Permissions

BNBot requests access to specific websites for multi-platform trend discovery. All scraping runs locally in your browser. Scraped content stays on your device — not sent to external servers. You can revoke access to any site in Chrome settings at any time.
