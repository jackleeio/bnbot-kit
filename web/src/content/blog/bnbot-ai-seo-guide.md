# BNBot AI SEO Guide

Search performance for AI products is still won by the teams that publish clear pages, explain real user outcomes, and keep technical SEO clean. BNBot fits that pattern well because it can convert product knowledge into consistent landing pages, articles, and distribution workflows.

## What BNBot should optimize first

1. Product pages that answer a specific search intent.
2. Blog posts that support adjacent queries and long-tail education.
3. Internal links that connect commercial pages and educational content.
4. Metadata that gives search engines and social platforms a stable summary.

## A simple operating model

Start with a keyword cluster around a real user problem such as AI SEO workflows, AI content repurposing, or crypto growth automation. For each cluster, BNBot should produce:

- One primary landing page with a conversion goal.
- Two or three supporting articles that address comparisons, tutorials, and FAQs.
- Reusable snippets for open graph descriptions, social copy, and internal links.

This structure gives the site a clear topical map instead of isolated pages.

## On-page rules that matter

- Keep one primary topic per URL.
- Write titles and descriptions for humans first, then tighten for search.
- Put the most specific promise near the top of the page.
- Use descriptive internal anchors so supporting pages pass context, not just clicks.

## Technical SEO checklist

- Canonical URLs should match the public route exactly.
- Open graph metadata should mirror the page topic and description.
- Sitemaps should include blog detail pages after publish.
- Dates and structured content should remain consistent across list and detail views.

## Where BNBot adds leverage

BNBot is most useful when it reduces the time between research and publication. Instead of treating SEO as a separate editorial function, the product can generate drafts, suggest internal links, and keep page metadata aligned with the article’s core angle.

That combination is what turns content velocity into a durable SEO system.
